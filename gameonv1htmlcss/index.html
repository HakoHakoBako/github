Instructions!

  • Welcome to Breaking Ice! Your goal is to reach 25 points as soon as possible
  • Each player rolls two dice during their turn. If either dice roll isn't a one, each roll adds to the player's score.
  • The player's turn continues until they roll a one, or they can choose to pass. If one of the dice rolls is a one, it will skip the player's turn.
  • However, if both of the player's dice rolls are a one, it will reset the player's score to zero. The player broke the ice!
  • Your goal is to reach 25 points and avoid breaking the ice. Good luck players!
