Research Form Design

Getting Started

January 19, 2026

This Medium article discusses best practices to follow when designing form pages. For any website or mobile application, the ultimate goal is to gather users to engage with the application. It’s important to create effective and easily accessible forms to keep the engagement of the user.

highly critical and important concept to make forms effective is not to make them complex. This can be done through utilizing different UX design choices. One example is showing the number of steps through a progress bar. Providing a clear number and minimizing the amount of steps for users can increase the likelihood of form completion.

Ultimately, most people don’t like to fill out forms in general. That is why it’s within best practice for designers to make forms easily accessible and straightforward for users. The more simple and easy it is to create well designed forms, the higher the likelihood it is for users to engage with the application.
